ABOUT ROYAL TRANSFERS

Royal Transfers is a 100% black owned company which specialises in airport transfers to and from all airports in Johannesburg including OR Tambo, Lanseria and Grand Central, for visitors, corporate companies and also door-to-door.


At Royal Transfers, we offer comprehensive ‘Meet and Greet’ service where our driver will meet you at arrivals with your name board and will assist with your luggage to the waiting vehicle. Our dedicated team will constantly monitor your flight for delays or early arrival to ensure that you are met promptly without any inconvenience. Our transfer service includes: ➢ Airport to hotels and guest houses ➢ Airport to houses ➢ Airport to game reserves ➢ Airport to meetings/conferences Just tell us where you want to go and we will take you there!
